About Me

I am Annabel. I feel very lucky to live in East London, where I can witness a vibrant multicultural microcosm that inspires my creativity. Recently, I returned to my old passion for writing, focusing on my experiences in the world and the hope I see emerging in this chaotic and contradictory environment. I aim for my blog to inspire others to reflect on their own thoughts.
